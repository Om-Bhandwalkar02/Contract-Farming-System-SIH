Features of कृषिमित्र

Connecting Dealers and Farmers

Seamlessly connect dealers and farmers through our app, creating a streamlined marketplace for agricultural produce.

Translation of Text

Enhance accessibility with automatic translation of text into multiple languages, ensuring users from different regions can use the app effectively.

Hassle-Free Smart Contract

Utilize smart contracts to automate agreements between farmers and dealers, reducing paperwork and simplifying the process.

Escrow Account

Ensure secure transactions with an escrow account that collects payment from the dealer before contract signing and processes payment to the farmer after successful delivery.

Dedicated Tutorial Section

Access comprehensive tutorials to guide users on how to effectively use the app, ensuring a smooth onboarding process.

User-Friendly Dashboard

Manage contracts, payments, and communications from an intuitive dashboard designed for ease of use and efficient navigation.

Frequently Asked Questions

The Assured Contract Farming System is a platform that facilitates agreements between farmers and buyers, ensuring market stability by securing guaranteed buyers for agricultural produce.

The translation feature automatically converts text into multiple languages to enhance accessibility for users from different regions.

A smart contract is a self-executing contract with the terms of the agreement directly written into code. It automates the execution and enforcement of agreements between parties.

An escrow account holds the payment from the dealer until the contract is signed and then releases the payment to the farmer upon successful delivery of goods.

The tutorial section can be accessed from the main menu. It provides step-by-step guides and video tutorials to help you get started with the app.
